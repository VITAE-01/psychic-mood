Participant Information & Consent

Welcome to the activty and mood study — a research project exploring how physical activity relates to emotional wellbeing in adults.

The aim is to design and implement a system that help users to visualize how their physical activity patterns may relate with mood over time.

  • Participation is voluntary.
  • You can withdraw at any time without any consequences.
  • Your data will be used solely for research purposes.
  • We will NOT collect any personally identifiable information.
  • We will ask you to share your behavioural beliefs and attitudes towards physical activity daily.
  • We will ask about your physical activity when you use the app by reporting your daily activity levels.
  • We will also ask you to complete brief mood surveys after each activity session on a daily basis.
  • Optional demographic information will help us better understand the diverse perspectives of our participants.
  • All data is anonymized and securely stored, ensuring your privacy is protected.

The study has received ethical approval from the University of Greater Manchester Research Ethics Committee

By participating in this study, you acknowledge that you have read and understood the information provided about the research.

You voluntarily agree to participate in this study by providing self‑reports about behavioural belief, physical activity and mood.

Your participation is crucial for advancing our understanding of the research topic, and we appreciate your contribution to this important work.
